How to Make Perfect Pancakes

Even little Pickles can make perfect pancakes. Here's how it's done...

Ingredients

  • 50g butter
  • 110g plain flour
  • 200 ml milk
  • 70 ml water
  • 2 large eggs
  • Pinch of salt

Method

  1. Melt the butter in a crepe pan over a medium-low heat (number 2 or 3).
  2. Put the flour in a mixing bowl and slowly pour in the milk, whisking all the time to get rid of any lumps.
  3. Now whisk in the water, followed by the eggs, the melted butter, and a pinch of salt. Your pancake batter is now ready.
  4. Put the buttery pan back on the hob and turn the heat up to medium-high (number 4).
  5. Spoon a ladleful of the pancake mixture into the pan – then tilt the pan from side to side to swirl the mix right up to the edges.
  6. After a minute or two, shake the pan to loosen the pancake. Now flip it over using a spatula – or toss it if you're feeling brave!
  7. Cook for another minute or so, then shake the pancake out of the pan onto a plate. Your first perfect pancake is now ready to eat!
  8. Repeat steps 4-7 with the remaining batter – you should get about 6 perfect pancakes from this recipe.
