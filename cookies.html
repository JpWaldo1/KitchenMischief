How To Make the Best Ever Chocolate Chip Cookies

Chewy, gooey, and yum-bum-booey! Chocolate chip cookies are the best! Here's how to make them...

Ingredients

  • 125g butter
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 75g brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tsp. vanilla extract
  • 150g plain flour
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 100g chocolate chips, or a bar of chocolate broken into chunks

Method

  1. Preheat the oven to 170ÂºC and line two baking sheets with baking parchment.
  2. Gently melt the butter in a small saucepan.
  3. Put both sugars into a mixing bowl, pour in the melted butter and beat well with a wooden spoon.
  4. Beat in the egg and vanilla extract.
  5. Sift the flour, baking powder and salt into the bowl and stir them in, then add the chocolate.
  6. Dot heaped spoonfuls of the mixture on to the baking sheets, leaving a good four centimetres in between each one as they really spread out.
  7. Place in the oven and bake for 8-10 minutes, until the cookies are turning pale golden brown.
  8. Remove from oven and leave them to cool for 5 minutes or until they have firmed up enough to be lifted onto a plate.
  9. Eat them quickly before you have to share them!

Variations

No chocolate? No problem! These cookies are just as good plain, or you could add nuts, raisins, or other dried fruits. You could even throw in some spices like ginger or cinnamon for something different.
